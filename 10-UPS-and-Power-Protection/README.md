# Power Protection Infrastructure

## Overview

A centralized power protection solution was implemented to protect critical infrastructure from power outages, voltage fluctuations, and unexpected shutdowns.

The solution utilizes an APC Easy UPS On-Line SRVS 3000VA system to provide continuous power protection for core business services.

---

# Objectives

- Protect critical infrastructure
- Prevent unexpected server shutdowns
- Reduce risk of data corruption
- Maintain business continuity
- Improve disaster recovery readiness

# Benefits of Online UPS Technology

Unlike line-interactive systems, online UPS technology continuously powers connected equipment through the inverter.

Benefits include:

- Zero Transfer Time
- Voltage Regulation
- Frequency Regulation
- Surge Protection
- Brownout Protection
- Improved Equipment Longevity
